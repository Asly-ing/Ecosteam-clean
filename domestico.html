Eco-friendly Cleaning and Maintenance

Keeping your home clean, safe, and well-maintained has never been easier. At Eco Steam Clean & Repairs, we combine eco-friendly deep cleaning with maintenance services to ensure a healthy and functional environment. Our high-pressure steam method removes dirt, bacteria, and allergens without leaving chemical residues, ensuring the best quality for your home and family. Additionally, we offer garden care and small repairs, providing a complete service for your home's maintenance.


Our services include:


  • General home cleaning: We perform deep cleaning of floors, walls, ceilings, doors, and windows. We remove dust, dirt, and allergens without leaving residues or generating moisture, ensuring a healthy and clean environment. Our team uses advanced techniques and eco-friendly products to guarantee effective and safe cleaning for the whole family.
  • Kitchen and bathroom cleaning: We disinfect countertops, appliances, tiles, and faucets. We remove grease, mold, and limescale using steam to ensure impeccable hygiene without harmful chemicals. Additionally, we make sure every corner of your kitchen and bathroom is spotless, providing a safe and healthy environment.
  • Furniture and upholstery cleaning: We offer deep cleaning of sofas, chairs, mattresses, and curtains. We remove stains, odors, and mites with steam, keeping your furniture fresh and like new. Our upholstery cleaning service is ideal for extending the life of your furniture and improving the air quality in your home.
  • Garden and exterior maintenance: We take care of lawn mowing, shrub pruning, weed removal, and cleaning of terraces and patios, ensuring your outdoor spaces look impeccable. Our team of professional gardeners keeps your garden in perfect condition all year round.
  • Basic repairs: We fix small issues like leaking faucets, perform basic electrical repairs, and maintain structures to ensure your home functions perfectly. Our repair service includes a wide range of solutions to keep your home in optimal condition.
  • Garage and storage room cleaning: We organize and disinfect storage spaces, removing dust, mold, and accumulated dirt, so you can make the most of every corner of your home. Our garage and storage room cleaning service helps you keep these spaces organized and free of contaminants.

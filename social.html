Social Housing Cleaning

Social and Community Housing

At Eco Steam Clean & Repairs, we are committed to providing deep ecological cleaning services for social and community housing in Amsterdam. We understand that shared spaces require constant maintenance to ensure a safe and healthy environment, especially for vulnerable people such as the elderly, children, and those with respiratory issues.

Cleaning and Disinfection Services for Social Housing

We use steam at 135 degrees to clean and disinfect without harsh chemicals, eliminating germs, mold, and dirt without leaving residues or excessive moisture. Our team works with professionalism and respect, ensuring an efficient service tailored to the needs of each community.


Our services include:


  • ✔Cleaning of common areas and shared facilities: Disinfection and sanitation of living rooms, dining rooms, shared bathrooms, and play areas.
  • ✔Deep kitchen cleaning: Degreasing and cleaning of appliances, ovens, countertops, tiles, and kitchen surfaces.
  • ✔Carpet and upholstery cleaning: Removal of stains and dirt from sofas, chairs, and carpeted areas without the need for toxic chemicals.
  • ✔Disinfection of touchpoints: Deep cleaning of handrails, doors, switches, bathrooms, and ventilation systems.
  • ✔Floor and tile cleaning: Removal of mold, embedded dirt, and residues using steam under pressure.
  • ✔Nebulization and environmental sanitation: Bionebulization service for the elimination of viruses and bacteria in the air, ensuring a healthier environment for residents.

With Eco Steam Clean & Repairs, your community will have a reliable, safe, and chemical-free cleaning service, ensuring cleaner and healthier spaces for everyone.

Factory Cleaning Services

Factories

At Eco Steam Clean & Repairs, we understand the importance of maintaining a clean, safe, and efficient work environment in factories and industrial facilities. Our eco-friendly cleaning service with high-pressure steam ensures the removal of waste, dirt, and contaminants without the use of harsh chemicals, which is essential for meeting hygiene and safety standards.

Cleaning and Maintenance Services for Factories

We use advanced technology to remove waste, grease, dust, and contaminants without the use of harsh chemicals, reducing health risks and environmental impact. Additionally, we perform infrastructure maintenance, installation of industrial furniture, shelving, and painting of workspaces, ensuring that the facilities are in optimal condition.


Our services include:


  • ✔Cleaning of production and manufacturing plants: Removal of waste, cleaning of machinery and work surfaces with effective and safe methods.
  • ✔Cleaning of chemical substances and spills: Use of steam to break down and remove oils, greases, and other industrial spills.
  • ✔Hygienic cleaning in food factories: Disinfection of production areas without leaving chemical residues, complying with the highest sanitary standards.
  • ✔Cleaning of conveyor belts: Safe removal of dirt and waste to prolong the life of the equipment and avoid contamination in production.
  • ✔Deep cleaning of floors: Treatment of surfaces such as tiles, cement, and other industrial areas, removing difficult stains and waste buildup.
  • ✔Cleaning of staff facilities: Disinfection of bathrooms, changing rooms, and rest areas, ensuring hygienic and comfortable spaces for workers.
  • ✔Cleaning of machinery and equipment: Use of steam to access difficult areas of the machinery, ensuring its proper functioning without damaging it.
  • ✔Maintenance and repairs: Installation of shelving, industrial furniture, and painting of spaces to improve the functionality and aesthetics of the work environment.

With Eco Steam Clean & Repairs, your factory will be clean, safe, and in perfect operating condition, without compromising the environment.

Medical Cleaning and Disinfection Services in Amsterdam

At Eco Steam Clean & Repairs, we offer specialized eco-friendly cleaning and disinfection services for medical facilities in Amsterdam. Professional Cleaning for Medical Facilities

We use an industrial steam cleaner to clean and disinfect medical buildings, providing a safer cleaning option for both clients and staff.


Our emphasis is on not using toxic chemicals and we pay special attention to minimizing cross-contamination. Our services are carried out with high-pressure hot steam and clean microfiber cloths, suitable for medical sanitization.


  • ✔Deep disinfection of consulting rooms and treatment rooms, eliminating contaminants from examination tables, desks, chairs, and frequently touched surfaces.
  • ✔Cleaning of common areas and waiting rooms, including furniture, windows, and floors.
  • ✔Sanitation of bathrooms and kitchens, ensuring the disinfection of faucets, countertops, and appliances.
  • ✔Disinfection of medical equipment and tools, using steam to eliminate microorganisms without damaging the equipment.
  • ✔Sanitation of ventilation systems, reducing the spread of harmful particles in the air.

In addition to cleaning, we take care of the maintenance and improvement of educational spaces, offering services such as:

  • ✔General repairs of doors, windows, and furniture, ensuring optimal maintenance in the facilities.
  • ✔Painting and renovation of rooms and consulting rooms, creating more pleasant and professional environments.
  • ✔Installation and adjustment of medical and administrative furniture, improving the functionality of the spaces.

We provide flexible services tailored to the needs of each medical center, guaranteeing quality, commitment, and an eco-friendly approach.

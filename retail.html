Cleaning Services (Shops, Shopping Centers)

At Eco Steam Clean & Repairs, we understand that proper cleaning and maintenance of your shop or shopping center are essential to provide a pleasant experience for your customers and ensure a hygienic and professional environment. We use high-pressure steam technology to clean and disinfect without harsh chemicals, safely and eco-friendly removing dirt, germs, and residues.


Our services include:


  • ✔Deep disinfection of hallways and common areas, ensuring hygiene on floors, walls, and ceilings.
  • ✔Sanitization of fitting rooms and racks, removing dirt and bacteria without damaging clothes or furniture.
  • ✔Disinfection of ATMs and touchpoints, such as buttons, screens, and outer frames.
  • ✔Deep cleaning of bathrooms and rest areas, including sinks, floors, mirrors, and baby changing stations.
  • ✔Removal of adhesives and tough residues without the need for harsh chemicals.

In addition to cleaning, at Eco Steam Clean & Repairs we offer maintenance and repair services to keep your business looking impeccable:

  • ✔Installation and assembly of commercial furniture, such as shelves, counters, and displays.
  • ✔Painting and renovation of spaces, improving the aesthetics and ambiance of the premises.
  • ✔Repairs of doors, windows, and structures, ensuring the functionality and safety of the establishment.

We adapt to the needs of each shop or shopping center, providing flexible and personalized services to keep your business in optimal condition.

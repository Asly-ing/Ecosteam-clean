Cleaning and Maintenance Services for Offices and Work Buildings

Our 135-degree steam cleaning system eliminates germs, allergens, and residues without the need for harsh chemicals, ensuring a safe environment for people with allergies or respiratory issues. Additionally, we complement our services with facility maintenance, furniture installation, and space painting to ensure every office looks professional and functional.


Our services include:


  • ✔Deep cleaning of floors and carpets: Removal of dust, residues, and allergens with industrial vacuuming and high-pressure steam. Treatment for carpets, wooden floors, and tiles, reaching difficult corners.
  • ✔Cleaning of ventilation and heating systems: Use of high-pressure steam to remove dust and bacteria accumulations in air ducts and heating systems.
  • ✔Sanitization of surfaces and workstations: Deep disinfection of desks, counters, and equipment without moisture risks, protecting electronic devices.
  • ✔Cleaning of shared areas: Disinfection of bathrooms, kitchens, break rooms, and common areas, removing grease, stains, and bacteria from all surfaces.
  • ✔Cleaning of office chairs: Safe treatment for fabric, metal, and plastic chairs with steam, without abrasive or toxic products. We also offer stain removal.
  • ✔Furniture maintenance and installation: Assembly and repair of desks, shelves, and other furniture to improve the functionality of the workspace.
  • ✔Painting and space renovation: We enhance the aesthetics of offices with professional painting to create a fresh and renewed environment.

With Eco Steam Clean & Repairs, your office will be spotless, free of contaminants, and properly maintained to ensure an optimal and healthy workspace.

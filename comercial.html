Commercial Services

At Eco Steam Clean & Repairs, we offer eco-friendly and effective commercial cleaning solutions in Central Amsterdam. We specialize in deep cleaning of offices, commercial premises, and corporate spaces, providing both contractual and non-contractual services according to the needs of each company.


Our services include scheduled office cleaning, facility maintenance, and sanitation of common areas with high-pressure steam, eliminating dirt and bacteria without the use of harsh chemicals. We also perform one-time cleanings in cases of renovations, store openings, and preventive maintenance.


At Eco Steam Clean & Repairs, we guarantee a spotless and healthy environment for your team and clients, contributing to a professional image and a more productive work environment.

Daycares and Schools

Disinfection of tables and chairs, equipment and children's toys, play areas, as well as common areas such as bathrooms and toilets. All without the need to use harmful chemicals, especially sensitive for children and babies.

Medical

Hygiene in medical environments is crucial for the safety of patients and healthcare personnel. We offer ecological disinfection of consulting rooms, waiting rooms, medical equipment, and patient care areas. Our high-pressure steam method eliminates viruses, bacteria, and other pathogens without leaving chemical residues, ensuring a clean and safe environment.

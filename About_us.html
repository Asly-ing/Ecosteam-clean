About Us

Welcome to Eco Steam Clean & Repairs, a company for deep ecological cleaning and repairs based in Amsterdam Center. We were founded with the mission to transform the cleaning industry, by providing sustainable and safe solutions for both our customers and the environment.


Our Mission

At Eco Steam Clean & Repairs, we believe that cleaning should not come at the expense of people's health or harm the environment. The traditional cleaning industry uses harsh chemicals that can leave toxic residues and affect indoor air quality. We want to change that. Our mission is to provide an ecological and effective alternative through steam cleaning and sustainable techniques. We specialize in removing bacteria, viruses, allergens, and dirt without the use of harmful chemicals, ensuring a safer environment for our customers and their families.


Through our services, we help households and businesses keep their spaces spotless, reduce their ecological footprint, and promote a healthier lifestyle. We are committed to providing high-quality, personalized service tailored to the specific needs of each customer. Additionally, we offer maintenance and repair solutions for homes and businesses, making spaces more functional, comfortable, and aesthetically improved.


Please note: A professional deep cleaning cannot be completed in less than two hours. We ensure thorough and effective service, maintaining the highest standards of quality.

At Eco Steam Clean & Repairs, we specialize in deep ecological cleaning and repair services with a safe, efficient, and sustainable approach.

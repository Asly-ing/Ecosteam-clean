Cleaning Services for Schools and Nurseries in Amsterdam

Cleaner, safer, and more functional educational spaces

At Eco Steam Clean & Repairs, we offer specialized deep cleaning and disinfection services for nurseries and schools in Amsterdam. Our eco-friendly approach ensures an environment free of bacteria, viruses, and allergens without the use of harsh chemicals, protecting the health of children, staff, and parents.


We use high-temperature steam technology, allowing us to perform deep and efficient cleaning in all educational areas, including:


  • ✔Cleaning of touchpoints and shared surfaces, such as desks, chairs, handrails, door handles, and reception areas.
  • ✔Sanitizing staff rooms and classrooms, ensuring the cleanliness of furniture, floors, window sills, and baseboards.
  • ✔Cleaning of sanitary facilities and kitchens, including faucets, food preparation surfaces, toilets, and tiles.
  • ✔Disinfection of toys and children's equipment, ensuring hygiene in play structures, art areas, and educational materials.
  • ✔Cleaning of patios and outdoor areas, covering swings, slides, and climbing structures.

In addition to cleaning, we take care of the maintenance and improvement of educational spaces, offering services such as:

  • ✔Installation of furniture and shelves, optimizing storage and organization in classrooms and offices.
  • ✔Painting of classrooms, hallways, and common areas, creating renewed and welcoming environments.
  • ✔Repairs of furniture and structures, including adjustment and restoration of doors, chairs, tables, and other essential elements.

We provide flexible services tailored to the needs of each institution, guaranteeing quality, commitment, and an eco-friendly approach.
